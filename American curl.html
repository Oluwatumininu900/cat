ABOUT AMERICAN CURL CAT

The American Curl is a cat characterised by its unusual ears, which curl back from the face toward the center of the back of the skull. The breed originated in Lakewood, California, due to a spontaneous mutation. Wikipedia Origin: United States Temperament: Curious, Adaptable, Companionable, Intelligent, Even Tempered, People-oriented Scientific name: Felis catus ACFA/CAA: standards: Short Hair, Long Hair TICA: standard

Balanced in temperament and not overly talkative, the breed communicates quietly, with gentle trilling and cooing sounds. But they do expect attention: it's important to make time for the American Curl as they don't enjoy being left alone for long periods of time or excluded from the activity of the household.

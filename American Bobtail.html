ABOUT AMERICAN BOBTAIL CAT

The American Bobtail is an uncommon breed of domestic cat which was developed in the late 1960s. It is most notable for its stubby "bobbed" tail about one-third to one-half the length of a normal cat's tail. Wikipedia Temperament: Dog-like, Energetic, Intelligent, Friendly, Tolerant, Playful Origin: United States Scientific name: Felis catus CFA: standard

Additionally, these cats are social and communicate with their owners by meowing or by jumping onto their laps. They don't like being left alone for extended periods. Fun fact: American Bobtails are so intelligent that they can escape from closed rooms or cages.

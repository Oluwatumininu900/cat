ABOUT ABYSSINIAN

The Abyssinian is a breed of cat with a distinctive "ticked" tabby coat, in which individual hairs are banded with different colours. They are also known simply as Abys. The first members of the breed to be exhibited in England were brought there from Abyssinia, hence the name. Wikipedia Temperament: Curious, Sensible, Loyal, Intelligent, Willful, Extroverted Scientific name: Felis catus Origin: Ethiopia, South East Asia TICA: standard

Cats are known for their ability to stay home alone for a long time, but Abyssinian cats need you to be around. Still, don't let this scare you away — they can stay calm for many hours, but you may not be able to leave them on their own while on vacation.
